the eternal ocean

Steel buoy, rope, plexiglass box, contact microphones, anchors, ballast in sandbox, eight-channel sound (14:32 continuous loop), light. Granoff Center for the Creative Arts, Providence, RI. 2017.

In the eternal ocean, an ever-present negotiation between human and nature is materialized. Directly adapted from the final challenge of Survivor Palau, the eternal ocean centers on a built structure that both produces and is the product of human struggle. Yet, it is difficult to discern where the body ends and the machine begins; to distinguish between humanity and the earthly sphere to which it is bound.
