An Arctic Timeline

Web application. 2018.

An Arctic Timeline was launched by the Climate Museum to accompany their first exhibition, In Human Time. This interactive web application presents an overview of climate history, human exploration of the Arctic, and Arctic culture. By scrolling down the "ice core," viewers of An Arctic Timeline are confronted with the profound entanglement of humans and climate.

As project lead, I was responsible for most design, research, writing, and web development of An Arctic Timeline. Lisa Goulet, Claudia Villar, and Miranda Massie contributed writing on culture and exploration. Kevin Cadena contributed mobile development.
