An Arctic Timeline

Web application. 2018.

An Arctic Timeline is soon to be launched by the Climate Museum to accompany their first exhibition, In Human Time. The interactive web application presents an overview of climate history, human exploration of the Arctic, and Arctic culture. By scrolling down the "ice core," viewers of An Arctic Timeline are confronted with the profound entanglement of humans and climate – reflected in part by the artifacts that we produce together.

As project lead, I was responsible for the design, research, writing, production, and web development of An Arctic Timeline. Lisa Goulet and Claudia Villar contributed writing on culture and exploration. Kevin Cadena contributed mobile development.
