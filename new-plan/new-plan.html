A New Plan to Capture Greenhouse Gas

Hardcover book, 8.75 x 11.25", 60 pages.

Published February 2023, Science for the People.

A New Plan to Capture Greenhouse Gas documents how growth-minded politicians, industrialists, and engineers have spent decades advocating for CO2 removal, at the expense of real climate solutions. Surprising stories surface: a failed “CO2 dump” experiment off the coast of Hawaii, an MIT partnership with the state-run oil giant Saudi Aramco, a campaign to deregulate pollution on the high seas, and more.

These stories are anchored by two drawing series, the Green Man and the London Convention Drawings. In each series, important documents from the decades-long history of CO2 disposal have been annotated, drawn over, and arranged into timelines. The book also features an introduction, an essay, and a conversation with the physicist and activist Ansar Fayyazuddin.
