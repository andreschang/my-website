the eternal ocean

Steel buoy, waterproof box, contact microphones, rope, anchors, ballast in sandbox, seven-channel sound (14:32 continuous loop), light. Granoff Center for the Creative Arts, Providence, RI. 2017.

In the eternal ocean, an ever-present negotiation between human and nature is materialized. Directly adapted from the final challenge of Survivor Palau, the eternal ocean centers on a built structure that both produces and is the product of human struggle.
