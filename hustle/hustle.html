Hustle!

Performance. 2016.

After constructing a framework, I engaged in a physical challenge. I began on one side of the ladder with only one rung at the top; on the other side was a mud pit with metal rungs entrenched within. I hurdled over the ladder to retrieve each rung, progressively building the ladder over time and tracing my movement through the spread of mud.

Filmed and edited by Cole Moore.
